Rates/Packages

$159

At this price, you get a half day on the river! Hold on tight as you experience class II-IV rapids! Lunch is provided on the river and so is dessert! But only if your raft guide brings it! So you better hope you get a good one!

$179

At this price you get to spend 3/4 of your day with us, passing through class II-IV rapids! Lunch is also provided and you get to start further up the river so that you can enjoy more fun in the sun and some good surfing spots!

At this price, you'll get to spend all day on the river! At about 7 hours of fun, you'll traverse the entire upper gorge of this powerful river with some great variety! Rafts will traverse every class of rapid from I-V, enjoying breathtaking scenery with as well as lunch!
